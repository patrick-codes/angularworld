ABOUT US - CLUB HISTORY AND PURPOSE

SHORT HISTORY AND INSPIRATION

Angular is a term coined from the word angle. Angular is a concept of promoting positivity and progress. This concept is built around the life of mankind and the inner desire of progress in everyone, that man has to carry on in life however happy or sad he is. The concept of Angular is meant to create awareness that a persons ability to ignite the positive energy within him, is the key to overcome every obstacle or situation he is going through. So this concept of the club is meant is ignite the inner desire of people to stand up for growth and positive Development.

Since 2022 October, the first institutional chapter of the club has been registered and approved in Aamusted and had been fully operational since May 2024. Mr. Boakye Manu Joshua who is the Founder of the program is an explorer, a writer, an activist and a dedicated entrepreneur who aims at building this program as a business organization to inspire generations and nations globally.

PURPOSE OF THE CLUB

PURPOSE

Angular World Club is a program designed as a social club by a group of dedicated Ghanaian entrepreneurs and youth activist to promote Tourism, Arts and Culture. As a social club, we aim at training and grooming the members of the club through modeling and mentorship to project Africanism and preserve Human Heritage.

Our main purpose is to build a community of explorers, event organizers, activist and also raise a generation of young dedicated entrepreneurs who aims at changing their lives, that of their families, communities and nations at large. This program is created to build and implement community development programs to support the harmonious Living in Ghanaian Elite communities (Estates/SSNIT Comm.) and Traditional Communities(Towns/Villages), mentor students in both the Basic schools(JHS level) and Second Cycle institutions(SHS/TVET) whilst Creating Skills Training and Entrepreneurial Programs to support people in vocation within the African communities. We are working on building community gigs and talents exhibition platforms with the idea to improve the average African/Ghanaian social life and basic skills development.
